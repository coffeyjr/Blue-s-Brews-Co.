BREWS

A cozy coffee shop that doubles as a beer bar, featuring 16 rotating taps of the best local beers and a menu of specialty coffee drinks. Whether you're a coffee connoisseur or a beer enthusiast, this is the spot for you.

FOOD

Savor the perfect start to your day with a delicious breakfast treat from our cafe, offering a variety of freshly-baked bagels, satisfying breakfast sandwiches, and much more to jumpstart your morning.

EVENTS

Experience the best of your community at our coffee shop, a gathering place for good brews, good company, and memorable events. From seasonal markets showcasing local vendors to creative workshops and more, there's always something happening here.

About Us

Starting in March of 2020 inside of a cafe converted 1957 Airstream trailer, Blue's Brews - named after Colson's 6-year-old Great Dane, Blue - was born. Opening up shop in the parking lot of Frannie's Vegan Cafe in Boones Creek, it would eventually move out to downtown Johnson City!
